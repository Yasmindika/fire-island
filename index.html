Adventures in Fire Island

,

It was fun walking in Fire Island, especially since my friends were around and we played games as we walked. We made lots of tiktoks and took many pictures for memory.

We had carried enough food, so we decided to have a picnic while looking at the beautiful scenery. We ate the food and had a bonfire near the beach. this was one of the best experience.
